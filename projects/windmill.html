Windmill Cellar: Streamlining Liquor Discovery

Windmill Cellar is a mobile solution that enables users to seamlessly discover and purchase liquor products. Developed for a UAE-based client, this app was built with Flutter and Dart to offer a smooth, cross-platform experience. By optimizing the user interface and performance, the app offers an enhanced experience over the website, improving user engagement and accessibility.
